Time series anomaly detection using Machine learning. Application to predictive maintenance in embedded systems.

work in progress

Hi I am Mansour MAYAKI. I am 3rd year phd student in computer science at Université Côte d'Azur and INRIA in France. I am currently working on time series anomaly detection using deep neural networks and application to predictive maintenance in embedded systems. My reasearch works are in collaboration with NXP Semiconductors : we aim to build high dimensional time series analysis solutions for predictive maintenance capable of operating on chips with reduced storage and computing capabities.

My reasearch interest are

  • Machine learning and Statistical methods for
    • Drift Detection
    • Anomaly detection
    • Time series analysis
  • Embedded AI: Collaboration with NXP Semiconductors

About me

After the baccalaureate, I obtained an engineering degree in statistics at ENSAE (Dakar, Senegal). Then I obtained a scholarship to continue at École Nationale de la Statistique et de l'Analyse de l'Information ( ENSAI, Rennes, France) where I obtained an engineering degree in statistics and data science. In parallel I obtained a master's degree in fundamental mathematics at Rennes 1 University (Rennes, France). I am currently preparing my phd in computer science and Artificial intelligence at Université Côte d'Azur.
